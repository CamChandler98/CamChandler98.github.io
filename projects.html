Rythm Realm

Rhythm Realm is an innovative audio processing system that integrates C++ programming with UE5 blueprinting. It takes PCM encoded audio data, converts it to numerical representations, and performs a Constant-Q transform to extract frequency information. By utilizing SIMD operations and aligned memory allocation, Rhythm Realm optimizes performance for real-time processing, visualizing frequency data, and manipulating 3D objects. It's a groundbreaking step towards interactive audio visualization and manipulation.

Built with C++, Unreal, Blender.

HeartString

HeartString is an alternative take on social media, meant to invoke the feeling of connecting with a stranger somewhere unexpected, like on the bus or in line. Users make anonymous posts called "Hearts" and from the replies on these posts, users can choose one person to connect with. after connecting two users are no longer anonymous to each other and can see which hearts belong to their connections.

Built with CSS, HTML, React, Redux, JavaScript, Python, Flask, Flask-SQLalchemy, PostgreSQL Socket.io, AWS, and Styled-Components
